About Virginia

Virginia, officially the Commonwealth of Virginia, is a state in the Southeastern and Mid-Atlantic regions of the United States. It is bordered by Maryland to the northeast, the Atlantic Ocean to the southeast, North Carolina and Tennessee to the south, Kentucky to the west, and West Virginia to the northwest. Virginia is the 12th-most populous state in the United States, with over 8.6 million residents.

Virginia's capital is Richmond; its largest city is Virginia Beach.
