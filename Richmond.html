Welcome to the Capital

Richmond, VA, exudes a captivating blend of Southern charm and modern vitality, making it a dynamic and inviting city to explore. With its historic significance as the former capital of the Confederacy, Richmond offers visitors and residents alike a glimpse into America's past through its well-preserved architecture, museums, and monuments. Beyond its historical heritage, Richmond boasts a thriving arts and cultural scene, with numerous galleries, theaters, and festivals showcasing local talent and creativity. From its scenic parks and outdoor recreational opportunities to its diverse dining options and vibrant neighborhoods, Richmond captivates with its endless possibilities and warm hospitality..

Quick Facts about Richmond

- City Population: 229,395 (2022).
- Incorporated in 1742.
- Located in the Piedmont Region on Virginia.
- Mixture of Urban and Suburban areas.
- Average income $34,975 (State average $42,496)
